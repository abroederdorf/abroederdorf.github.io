Activity Categories:

  • Hike (yellow): Does not matter the destination as long as no technical skills were required
  • Climb (green): Technical skills required to reach the summit (i.e. use of ice axe or rope). Some snow climbs would be considered hikes during the summer
  • Crag (orange): Within climbing I have separated cragging, those areas where I solely rock climbed
  • Backcountry Ski (white): Heading up something on my skis
