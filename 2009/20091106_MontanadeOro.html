Montańa de Oro 11-6-09

Cory was hoping to hike the PGE trail but the trailhead closed at 5 PM so we instead headed for Montańa de Oro and the sand dunes. As always, I grabbed my GPS and stuck it in my pocket. After a short trail through some brush, we came upon the sand dunes. It’s a good little challenge climbing up the dunes - we found running worked best. There was a large group of people where Cory normally went so we explored more of the dunes close to the coast. We found a good spot and sat to enjoy the ocean below. After turning off my head lamp and his flashlight and letting our eyes adjust, it was interesting to watch the waves come in and the large white surf sections left behind. Unfortunately it was a cloudy night so there were no stars to gaze at. On the way back to the car we had a little bit more fun in the dunes by jumping off of a steep side into the sand below. Once back at the car, Cory emptied his shoe creating a personal sand box, before heading back to SLO.
