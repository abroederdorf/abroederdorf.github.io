2012 Year in Review

As most know me, they know that I like numbers and data. I thought I'd post my yearly numbers seeing as we're bringing in a new year, the year of the volcanoes! I'm hoping to check several Cascade volcanoes off of my list in 2013, including Mt Baker, Mt Rainier, Mt Adams, Mt Hood, Mt St Helens, and if we have time after tagging those, maybe a few others in Oregon. Hopefully I'll get some classic rock routes checked off as well, and maybe a few other peaks that have eluded me thus far in addition to another couple of races.
